Epic 10 Goal:

Make the application more production-ready by adding consistent observability, safe AI telemetry, global error handling, correlation ID propagation, health checks, operational documentation, and Application Insights readiness.

This epic should demonstrate enterprise-grade supportability, traceability, diagnosability, responsible AI telemetry, and Azure cloud-readiness.
